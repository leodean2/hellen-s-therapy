About Hellen Counseling

Learn about our mission, values, and professional team

Our Mission

At Hellen Counseling, we are dedicated to providing compassionate, professional mental health services to individuals and families in our community. Our mission is to create a safe, supportive environment where clients can explore their challenges and develop strategies for lasting change.

Our Approach

We believe in a client-centered approach that respects each individual's unique experiences and perspectives. Our therapists draw from evidence-based practices including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), and mindfulness techniques to help clients achieve their therapeutic goals.

Our Team

Our licensed therapists bring years of experience and specialized training in various areas of mental health. Each team member is committed to ongoing professional development to ensure we provide the highest quality care.
